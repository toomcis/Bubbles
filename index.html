Getting started

Up and running in seconds.

No setup, no dependencies. Just download and drop.

01

Grab the latest release

Head to github.com/toomcis/Bubbles → Releases and download Bubbles.zip.

02

Open Lively Wallpaper

If you don't have it yet, grab Lively Wallpaper — it's free and open source.

03

Drag and drop

Drag Bubbles.zip straight into the Lively window. No extracting needed.

04

Customize to your heart's content

Use Lively's settings panel to tweak colors, speed, thickness, and more. Try the live preview above to find your perfect settings first.

Reference

Every knob, explained.

All properties are accessible from Lively's built-in settings panel once the wallpaper is installed.

Speed

How fast the blobs animate. 0 freezes them completely. Higher values give a more energetic, fluid feel.

Line Color

The color of the blob outlines or fill. Works with any color — try pastels against a dark background.

Background Color

The empty space between blobs. Pair with Line Color for maximum contrast or go subtle with similar tones.

Density

Controls how many blobs appear. 0.5 is the densest. Moving toward 0 or 1 thins them out — it's not linear, think of it as tuning a frequency.

Thickness

Width of the line band. Slide to maximum to switch into filled mode — solid blobs instead of outlines.

Uniform Line Width GPU HEAVY

Computes pixel-accurate border width so lines stay consistent everywhere — no thin pinches on tight curves. Uses extra GPU.

Interior Brightness

Only active in Uniform mode. Controls how bright the blob interiors are. 0 = background color, 1 = line color.
